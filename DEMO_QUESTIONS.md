# Chatbot Demo Questions

Here are 10 easy and creative questions to showcase the AMAST Sales Chatbot during your product demo:

## 1. **Sales Overview**
"Show me total sales revenue for this month"

## 2. **Top Performers**
"What are the top 5 best-selling products by revenue?"

## 3. **Outlet Performance**
"Which outlet has the highest sales this year?"

## 4. **Time-based Analysis**
"How many invoices were created last week?"

## 5. **Product Insights**
"Show me products with low stock levels"

## 6. **Customer Analysis**
"Which customers have the most orders?"

## 7. **Revenue Trends**
"What is the total revenue by month for this year?"

## 8. **Delivery Performance**
"Show me delivery orders that are pending"

## 9. **Product Categories**
"What are the top product categories by sales volume?"

## 10. **Business Intelligence**
"Give me a summary of today's sales performance"

---

## Bonus Questions (More Advanced)

- "Compare sales between this month and last month"
- "Show me all credit notes issued this month"
- "What is the average order value?"
- "Which products have the highest profit margin?"
- "Show me sales by price group"

---

## Tips for Demo:

1. **Start simple** - Begin with question #1 to show basic functionality
2. **Show variety** - Mix different types of queries (sales, products, outlets)
3. **Highlight intelligence** - The chatbot understands natural language
4. **Show SQL** - If available, demonstrate that users can see/edit the generated SQL
5. **End strong** - Use question #10 to show comprehensive analysis

## Expected Results:

- The chatbot will identify relevant database tables
- Generate SQL queries automatically
- Execute queries and return formatted results
- Provide insights in natural language
- Display data in tables and charts (if applicable)
